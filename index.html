🎯 Project Overview

Trakito is a revolutionary productivity app designed to help users become more conscious of their time usage through intuitive activity tracking and visual time management.

💡 The Problem

In today's fast-paced world, people often lose track of how they spend their time. Traditional productivity apps are either too complex, require constant internet connectivity, or lack the visual simplicity needed for daily use.

Users need a tool that:

  • Works offline without requiring accounts
  • Provides instant visual feedback
  • Helps build better time awareness habits
  • Respects privacy by storing data locally

🚀 The Solution

Trakito transforms time tracking into a simple, visual grid-based experience that makes time management intuitive and enjoyable.
