Privacy Policy

Early Access Email Collection

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ilusoft.fun ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you sign up for early access to our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Polish RODO.

2. Information We Collect

For the purpose of providing early access to our application, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Your email address to send you notifications about early access availability
  • Consent Timestamp: The date and time when you provided consent for data processing

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To send you notifications when early access becomes available
  • To communicate important information about the beta testing program
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our early access program

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on your explicit consent, which you provide by checking the consent checkbox when signing up for early access. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or until you withdraw your consent. Specifically:

  • Your email address will be retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • We will delete your data within 30 days of receiving a deletion request
  • If early access is not launched within 2 years, we will delete your data

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Secure hosting and infrastructure

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and RODO, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent at any time

8. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to help us manage our early access program. These services include:

  • Email service providers for sending notifications
  • Analytics services to understand usage patterns
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers

All third-party services are carefully selected and are required to maintain the same level of data protection as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

9. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that appropriate safeguards are in place.

10. Children's Privacy

Our early access program is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website
  • Sending you an email notification if you have provided consent

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: ilusoft.contact@gmail.com

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

Quick Actions

To exercise your rights or make requests regarding your personal data, you can:

  • Email us at ilusoft.contact@gmail.com
  • Use the unsubscribe link in any email we send you
